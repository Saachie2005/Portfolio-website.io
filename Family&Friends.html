My People
I respect and love the people
around me with my whole
heart. I would do anything for
my friends and family. Having
so many friends becomes a
task sometimes keeping in
mind to meet all of them.
However, they bring joy to my
life. Having so many friends
does not mean I do not spend
time with my family. My bond
with my family is stronger than
anyone could imagine. I would
not be who I am without them.
